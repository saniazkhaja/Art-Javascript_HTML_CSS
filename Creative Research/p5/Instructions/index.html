Instructions

In this game, you will get to adjust multiple factors to see how crime in a city is affected by them! Adjust each factor by sliding the bar similar to the one in the image. You will get to see how the factors affected the amount of crime in the city and how a type of crime is affected by the factors. When done, click the finish button and you will get to see where I got the information from and can find out more about how I calculated it all. Experiment and have fun!
